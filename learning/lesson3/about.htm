About Gems by Langdon

James Langdon has been cutting and polishing precious and semi-precious gems for several years from a small workshop in Brighton.
To give his work a unique selling point he developed a concave faceting rig. He is (as far as is presently known) the only faceter making concave cuts in the South of England.
